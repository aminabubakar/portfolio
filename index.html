Who I Am

About

"I believe learning is strongest when students are given space to question, investigate, collaborate, and make sense of ideas for themselves."

I am a Physics educator, IB Programme and Workshop Leader, and Apple Distinguished Educator with 20 years of teaching experience across Singapore, Indonesia, and China. My work is grounded in inquiry-based learning and the thoughtful use of technology to deepen student understanding. As artificial intelligence becomes part of everyday learning, I am especially interested in how teachers can use AI to support feedback, reflection, and student agency without losing the human relationships at the heart of education. My experiences across different school systems have shaped me into a teacher who values adaptability, cultural awareness, and learning that feels purposeful to students.
