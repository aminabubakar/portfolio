Learning
Teachers use inquiry-based teaching strategies and learning engagements. (0403-01-0100)
We utilized the PASCO scientific open speaker and resonance tube in a recent class activity. This innovative tool allowed our students to see and hear the phenomenon of standing waves, transforming what is often an abstract concept in physics into a tangible, relatable experience. It was particularly enlightening to relate this to musical instruments, like the clarinet one of our students plays, offering a practical context to the theory.
Purpose
The school develops a mission, philosophy and/or strategy that includes a holistic approach to education that goes beyond academic development and encourages awareness beyond the individual and the immediate community. (0101-01-0200)
In every practice, game, and team meeting, we strive to instill a sense of responsibility, teamwork, and respect among our players. We emphasize the importance of understanding and empathy, encouraging our athletes to consider the perspectives of others, from their teammates to the wider community. Our goal is to cultivate not just skilled soccer players, but well-rounded individuals who are aware of their roles and responsibilities in a connected world.
